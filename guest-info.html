WHERE TO STAY

Accomodations

Since we anticpate guests will be exploring the great state of Alaska during the wedding week, we have not reserved blocks of rooms at any hotel.

If you plan to stay in Anchorage for a few nights at the beginning of your stay, we recommend being close to the Airport or Downtown Anchorage -- those will be the most convenient locations for most activities.

The wedding itself will be held at Girdwood's Crow Creek Mine. For the days leading up to the wedding, most activities will take place in Girdwood. The town has a variety of lodging options including house rentals, condos, and a hotel.

Anchorage will have more to provide in terms of city activities (museums, restaurants, shopping, parks) but other locations will have more outdoor activities and sights, so choose your accomodations based on what you hope to do on this trip! We highly encourage you to explore other cities in Alaska such as Seward, Homer or Talkeetna.

SEE ALASKA!

Anchorage is only a tiny slice of Alaska. We highly encourage you to take this opportunity to explore this great state. Go on a fishing charter or a glacier cruise out of Seward or Homer. Drive over to Prince William Sound. Check out Kenai Fjord National Park or Resurrection bay. Try out a flightseeing tour. Go hiking, kayaking or biking just about anywhere. If you are feeling very adventerous, go to Denali Park or take an Alaskan cruise. If you need more ideas or recommendations check out Travel Alaska or just ask us!
