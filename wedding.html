WEDDING & RECEPTION


Location


The wedding and reception will take place at the Crow Creek Mine in Girdwood, AK on July 12, 2014 at 4:30 pm.

The Crow Creek Mine
601 Crow Creek Rd
Girdwood, AK 99587

We kindly request that our event remain adults only.

Crow Creek Mine is a family owned and operated Historical and Recreational Gold Mine. Nestled in a very beautiful mountain setting, Crow Creek Mine offers the oldest buildings in the Anchorage area, as well as some of the most popular recreational gold mining in all of Alaska.


Driving Directions


From Anchorage:

Drive south, 35 miles on the Seward Highway to Girdwood (Alyeska). Take a left at the gas station / Alyeska Highway for Girdwood. Continue on Alyeska Highway for 1.9 miles and just before the bridge crossing you come to Crow Creek Road on your left. Follow Crow Creek Road for 2.7 miles (becomes gravel road) until you come to the Crow Creek Mine (there will be a big sign), take a right and drive down to their parking lot.

ADDITIONAL INFO


Attire


We want our guests to have a good time, without worrying too much about what you wear. Please dress to your preference and comfort.

Keep in mind that summer in Alaska is a bit cooler then what you may be used to. Be prepared for temperatures between 50 - 60 F, possible rain, and a lot of mosquitos. We will be providing shelter and heaters at the reception, but please make sure you will be comfortable.
