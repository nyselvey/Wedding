NATASHA

RUSSELL


Russell grew up in Anchorage, AK with his parents Molly and Bret, and his two younger siblings Don and Celia. He graduated from Oregon State University with a bachelor of science in Computer Science. After graduation he moved to San Francisco to continue working at a tech company.
